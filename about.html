A day in the life...

My full name is Mac Rooney Wynne. Apparently my owners could not decide on either Mac or Rooney for my name so they gave up and kept them both.. I typically am only called Mac unless my owners are mad at me then I get the full name treatment. I was born in Greenfield, Massachusetts on January 11, 2011. My parents were Piper and Rupert.

My favorite things are:

    Food
    Sleep
    Frisbees

My favorite food is watermelon. My favorite place to sleep is in one of my beds in the sun. Click the link to see my favorite frisbee. A perfect day for me is to wake up early and have breakfast right away. Then go out for a brisk walk. After my walk I have a quick snack and then settle in for a nap. After my nap I go outside for a spirited game of chasing the frisbee. The more frisbies the better! I can chase 2 or even three at a time. After playing it's time for a long afternoon nap before dinner. After dinner I hang out and wait for everyone to go to bed so I can go with them.
